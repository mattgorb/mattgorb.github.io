Wind Direction Prediction

Wind Turbines have dozens of sensors that capture data on various environmental and machine component variables. I came across a public dataset from the company Engie in France that presents data for four wind turbines from 2013-2016. I explored Time Series Forecasting on wind direction using Support Vector Regression and single and multivariate LSTM Recurrent Neural Network modeling.
