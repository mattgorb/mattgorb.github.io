Snowmass

On 12/29/17 I left Purgatory Trailhead and hiked solo through the night into some of Colorado's most remote backcountry. I bivouacked for 3 hours at treeline and got up early to hit the four big summits surrounding the Chicago Basin. It turned into a pretty intense 36 hour trip. Lessons learned: Boil and don't just melt snow below treeline for drinking, take care of your feet, and don't cache your backpack in the dark without marking it.
